Common questions, answered

Can't I just figure this out by launching more creatives?
You can — most coaches do. The problem isn't volume, it's signal. Without a diagnosis of which ads are fatigued, which are stable, and which angles have room to run, you're throwing creative at a wall. The plan replaces guesswork with a structured rotation calendar built from your actual winners and audience response patterns.
My ad account is small — is this worth it?
Built for coaches and agencies spending $3K to $50K/month. Smaller accounts often see CPL improvements faster because every dollar counts more. If you're under $2K/month, we'll tell you on the strategy call.
How long until my CPL actually drops?
Most accounts see measurable CPL improvement within 3–5 weeks once the first new angles are live and out of the learning phase. The bigger win — scaling without CPL breaking — typically lands between weeks 6 and 10.
Is this just Lucas AI doing everything automatically?
No. Lucas AI handles analysis, angle generation, fatigue scoring, and weekly monitoring. Lucas (the human) shows up weekly to review your account and make judgment calls the AI can't. It's a hybrid by design.
